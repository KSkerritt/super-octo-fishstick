Vision

Collect Yuh Carnival is a service offered to YUMA masqueraders, giving you the opportunity to have your costumes delivered to you without the hassle or time spent waiting in line at the mas camp for collection

Goal

Our goal is to keep you out of the line so you can spend more time getting ready to pump and in the fete! Relax at home or get that extra sleep (you'll need it) and get your costume delivered to you. Missed your flight or late for costume pick up? Be assured that we can collect your costume and deliver to you.

Delivery Dates

Costume delivery dates will be based on YUMA's costume distribution schedule
