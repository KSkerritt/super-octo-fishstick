HOW IT WORKS

5 Easy Steps

  1. Click on “Schedule Delivery

  2. Enter all information including your name, Section, Desired Location for delivery along with a form of valid ID and your YUMA receipt

  3. Once your information is submitted correctly you will receive an email confirming the details of your delivery

  4. If the person collecting the costume is not the same as the one who registered, the person collecting will be required to print confirmation receipt along with authorisation letter and present it upon delivery
